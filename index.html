Conclusion

Contemporary Turkish e-commerce consumers‚Äîparticularly Gen Z and time-constrained professionals‚Äîoperate within a socially-mediated trust ecosystem that diverges from traditional funnel assumptions. The interviews indicate that community validation replaces brand authority, platform ecosystems displace search engines, and post-purchase experience quality drives loyalty more than product satisfaction alone. Security awareness exists without behavioral change, as consumers acknowledge risks but continue shopping.
